Number Porting

Check if your UK landline or national number can be ported to VoIP.

Enjoy all the benefits of VoIP while keeping your old business numbers with our simple number porting process!

Benefits of VoIP:

  • More Flexibility: VoIP allows you to manage calls from anywhere, on any device.
  • Lower Costs: Save money on communication expenses compared to traditional phone systems.

Number Porting Made Easy:

  1. Provide the numbers you wish to port (£25 per number ex-VAT).
  2. Submit the necessary documents:
    • A copy of a bill (including the number).
    • Proof of identity (passport or driver’s license).
    • Proof of address (bank statement).

Important:

  • Your full name and address should match exactly with the information on your current service provider’s records. If you have moved to a new location or changed your name, please let us know.
  • Do NOT cancel or change any services with your old number provider until the porting process is complete.

Switching to VoIP is hassle-free and ensures you retain your existing business numbers without interruption.

VoIP Numbers

Choose the right type of VoIP number for your business needs.

We offer a variety of VoIP numbers to suit different business needs:

Local 01 or 02 Numbers:

Purpose: Ideal for businesses that provide services within a specific geographic area.

Example Use Case:

  • Local Shops: Local shops use 01 or 02 numbers to establish a local presence and make it easier for nearby customers to reach them.
  • Estate Agencies: Estate agencies in a specific city, such as London, use local numbers to attract clients within the area and convey a sense of local expertise.

National 03 Numbers:

Purpose: Suitable for businesses that operate nationwide and want to offer a single, easily recognizable point of contact across the UK.

Example Use Case:

  • Consultancy Firms: National consultancy firms use 03 numbers to provide a consistent contact point for clients all over the UK, enhancing their national presence.
  • Customer Service Centers: Large customer service centers use 03 numbers to ensure that customers can contact them at standard geographic call rates, which are often included in call packages.

Mobile 07 Numbers:

Purpose: Used to give a more personal touch to business communications, often creating a sense of direct, personal connection.

Example Use Case:

  • Small Businesses or Freelancers: Entrepreneurs and small business owners use 07 numbers to give clients a direct line that can be answered personally, even on the go.

Benefits of Our VoIP Number Offerings:

  • Flexibility: Choose the type of number that best suits your business needs, whether it’s establishing a local presence, providing nationwide access, or creating a personal touch.
  • Cost Savings: VoIP numbers are typically more cost-effective compared to traditional phone lines, especially for businesses with high call volumes.
  • Professional Image: Enhance your business’s professional image with a variety of number options that align with your service area and client expectations.
  • Mobility: VoIP numbers can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection, ensuring you never miss a call, whether you’re in the office or on the move.

By offering these diverse VoIP number options, we help businesses connect more effectively with their customers, streamline their communication processes, and project the right image for their specific needs.
